Solutions to Air Pollution

Addressing air pollution through engineering solutions involves implementing emission control technologies, promoting electric vehicles and sustainable transportation, transitioning to renewable energy sources, and adopting green building practices. Smart urban planning and effective waste management are also crucial. A multi-pronged approach, combining these engineering solutions with supportive policies and public engagement, is vital in reducing air pollution and safeguarding air quality for future generations.
